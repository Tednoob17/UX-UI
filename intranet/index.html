What is a Piscine?

A Piscine (French for "swimming pool") is an intensive bootcamp format pioneered by schools like 42, Epitech, and Holberton. For several weeks, participants are immersed in hands-on projects, peer-learning, and self-paced progression.

This UX-UI Pool follows the same philosophy: you learn by doing, track your own progress, and level up through quests and projects.
